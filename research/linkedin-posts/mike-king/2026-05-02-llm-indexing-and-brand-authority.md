# LLM Indexing and Brand Authority
- **Author:** Mike King
- **Date:** 2026-05-02
- **URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/mikekingseo_ai-search-llm-indexing-activity-7324810294871920384-mKng
- **Note:** Breaks down the technical realities of how generative engines score and weight brand mentions.

If you think AI optimization is just about semantic density, you’re missing the bigger engineering picture. LLMs aren’t just looking at keywords; they are processing vectors and relying heavily on the pre-trained weights of brand entity nodes. 

To influence what an answer engine says about your brand, you need to dominate the corpus the model was trained on, plus the real-time context fetched via RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation). This means digital PR, high-authority backlink profiles, and explicit schema deployment are more critical than ever. If your brand doe have a strong entity signature in the knowledge graph, generative models will default to hallucinating your competitor's name instead of yours.
