# AI Search Readiness and Tracking

- **Date:** 2026-04-15
- **URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/aleydasolis_seo-ai-search-readiness-activity-7319401758202931201-xYzR
- **Note:** Focuses on frameworks for tracking AI share-of-voice and auditing content structure for LLM parsing.

Optimizing for AI Search (GEO/AEO) isn't a guessing game anymore; it requires an actual framework. When auditing for LLM engine visibility like Perplexity, Gemini, or OpenAI Search, we must track three core pillars: Mention Share, Source Variety, and Sentiment Alignment.

Are you being cited as the definitive answer, or just listed as a random link in the footnotes? To win in 2026, brands must audit their content layout to ensure LLMs can easily parse key answers. If your content is buried behind interactive JS elements or poorly structured tables, these models will simply pull facts from your competitors who format data cleanly. Stop waiting for standard GA4 traffic to show these trends—start building your own internal AI share-of-voice trackers today.
