Most AI SEO writing advice doesn't survive cross-vertical scrutiny. When analyzing writing signals across 7 verticals, metrics like word count and list density are highly dependent on your industry. For example, the signals that lift CRM/SaaS citation rates will actively hurt a Finance brand. However, there is one universal rule for AI citations: open your intro with a direct, declarative statement. No preamble, no context-setting, and definitely no questions. Just use the format "[X] is [Y]" or "[X] does [Z]". AI also heavily penalizes hedging in your opening paragraph, so strip out the qualifiers. Getting straight to the point is the only writing instruction that holds regardless of vertical, delivering a +14% aggregate lift in citations.
