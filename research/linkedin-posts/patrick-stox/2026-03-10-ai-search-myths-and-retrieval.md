Traditional SEO isn't dead, but the way we think about optimization has to evolve. AI Search and Perplexity-style answer engines don't magically guess answers; they rely on traditional information retrieval systems, indexing, and crawls. 

The biggest myth right now is that you can completely manipulate AI answers with hidden keywords or prompt injection tricks. In reality, LLM-based search engines prioritize factual consistency, source authority, and structured formatting. If your technical SEO setup prevents proper crawling or indexing, no amount of AI optimization will save your visibility. Focus on clear schemas, clean site architecture, and unmatched data accuracy.
